Radhey Shanti Foundation

About Us

Radhey-Shanti Foundation is a humanitarian non-profit initiative committed to transforming the lives of elderly individuals residing in old age homes by fostering self-sufficiency, dignity, and a renewed sense of purpose.

Anchored in the timeless philosophy of “Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam”—the world is one family—our work transcends mere caregiving...

Through a holistic approach that integrates vocational training, psychological support, creative enrichment, and wellness interventions...

At Radhey-Shanti Foundation, we envision a society where aging is not equated with decline, but embraced as a stage of wisdom...
